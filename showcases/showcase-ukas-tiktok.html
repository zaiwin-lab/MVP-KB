Key Website Features
🌐
Multilingual Including Jaku Iban
Full platform in English, Bahasa Malaysia, and Jaku Iban — the only government campaign platform in Sarawak to celebrate indigenous languages digitally. Cultural pride built in.
✓ Jaku Iban — First of Its Kind
📂
6-Category Registration System
Flexible multi-category entry — Tourism & Heritage, Lifestyle & Culture, Education & Innovation, Community & Inspiration, Environment & Sustainability, and Open Category. Inclusive for all Sarawakians.
✓ 1,248 Entries Received
⚙
Automated Entry Management Dashboard
Full admin dashboard with real-time entry tracking — total entries, pending review, approved, and rejected counts. Inline approval workflow with participant details and submission history.
✓ 1,062 Approved Automatically

Mobile-First Creative Design
Built mobile-first for today's content creators. TikTok-native aesthetics, bold visuals, and thumb-friendly layouts make it easy to register, share, and participate on any device.
✓ TikTok-Native UX

Uniting creators across all 82 Sarawak constituencies under one powerful movement — empowering individuals and communities to highlight the beauty, diversity, and progress of Sarawak.
