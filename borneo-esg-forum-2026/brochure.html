About the Forum

Borneo’s Premier ESG Dialogue

The Borneo ESG Forum 2026 is a landmark two-day regional conference dedicated to advancing Borneo’s green economy and ESG leadership. Convening 200+ policymakers, investors, corporates, SMEs, NGOs, and academics, the Forum creates the dialogue platform Borneo’s sustainability transition demands.

Anchored by Sarawak’s Sustainability Blueprint 2030 and the Sarawak Energy Transition Policy (SET-P), the Forum catalyses conversations that convert policy into action.
