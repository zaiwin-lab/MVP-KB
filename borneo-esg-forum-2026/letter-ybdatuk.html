Request for Official Patronage, Government Endorsement & Keynote Address — Borneo ESG Forum 2026

Yang Berhormat Datuk Dr Hazland,

We write with great respect and honour on behalf of the Organising Committee of the Borneo ESG Forum 2026, a landmark two-day regional sustainability conference scheduled for 14–15 October 2026 in Kuching, Sarawak. Themed “Scaling Borneo’s Green Economy for Sustainable Growth,” the Forum is designed to catalyse high-impact dialogue, forge strategic partnerships, and accelerate action across Borneo’s most critical environmental, social, and governance (ESG) priorities.

The Borneo ESG Forum 2026 is anchored by the recognition that Sarawak stands at an extraordinary inflection point in its sustainability journey. The launch of the Sarawak Sustainability Blueprint 2030 — Malaysia’s first state-level dedicated sustainability masterplan — and the landmark Sarawak Energy Transition Policy (SET-P) have positioned Sarawak as a leading voice in Southeast Asia’s green transition. The achievements to date are remarkable:

RM347B
Green Investments Mobilised
70%
Electricity from Renewables
72%
Carbon Cut Since 2010

Against this backdrop, the Borneo ESG Forum 2026 seeks to deepen and accelerate this momentum by convening policymakers, investors, corporates, SMEs, civil society, and academics across two days of substantive exchange. The Forum’s eight expert sessions address the most pressing themes in Borneo’s sustainability landscape — from green investment bottlenecks and nature-based solutions to responsible growth and combating greenwashing — areas where Sarawak’s emerging policy ecosystem makes it uniquely positioned to lead.

We are deeply aligned with MEESty’s mission of ensuring a clean energy and sustainable environment for Sarawak, and with Yang Berhormat’s own vision that “sustainability and economic growth are two sides of the same coin.” The Borneo ESG Forum 2026 is a natural extension of the regional dialogue that MEESty has championed through the International Energy Week and the Hydrogen Economy Forum — bringing the same spirit of convening excellence to the ESG and green economy agenda.

In this spirit, the Organising Committee respectfully requests the following from Yang Berhormat and MEESty:

  • Official Government Patronage — Sarawak state-level endorsement of the Forum, lending the credibility that befits a regional sustainability platform of this significance.
  • Keynote or Special Address — We would be deeply honoured to invite Yang Berhormat to deliver an Opening Keynote or Special Address, reinforcing Sarawak’s green economy leadership before a regional audience of 200+ delegates.
  • Institutional Partnership — An MEESty partnership designation, enabling co-branding and collaborative outreach to relevant government agencies, GLCs, and industry bodies.
  • Ministerial Nominee Participation — Should Yang Berhormat’s schedule not permit direct participation, we warmly welcome a nominated MEESty representative to participate as panelist or discussant.

The Borneo ESG Forum 2026 is a paid, curated event, designed to attract high-quality delegates who are serious practitioners in sustainability, green finance, and responsible development. Government endorsement will significantly amplify our ability to convene the right audience and achieve the policy dialogue outcomes that Sarawak’s sustainability agenda deserves. This forum is, at its core, a platform that serves Sarawak’s own strategic interests.

We would be greatly honoured to present a full proposal and attend a briefing at Yang Berhormat’s earliest convenience. Please do not hesitate to contact the Organising Committee at the details below to arrange a meeting at your preferred time and location.

We thank Yang Berhormat sincerely for your time and consideration, and for your continued leadership in advancing Sarawak’s green future. We look forward with great anticipation to the possibility of working together to make the Borneo ESG Forum 2026 a landmark moment for Borneo’s sustainability journey.
