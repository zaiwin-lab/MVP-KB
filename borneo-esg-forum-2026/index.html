About the Forum

Borneo’s Premier ESG Dialogue

A landmark two-day regional platform bringing together policymakers, investors, corporates, and communities to advance Borneo’s transition to a green, inclusive, and sustainable economy.

Set against Sarawak’s ambitious sustainability trajectory — including the Sarawak Sustainability Blueprint 2030 and the Sarawak Energy Transition Policy (SET-P) — this forum catalyses the conversations, partnerships, and action plans needed to scale Borneo’s green economy.

1

Promote practical ESG and sustainability dialogue across Borneo

2

Strengthen collaboration between public, private, and community stakeholders

3

Support green investment, innovation, and responsible development

4

Encourage scalable and impactful sustainability initiatives

5

Advance Borneo’s role in sustainable regional growth

Forum Focus Areas

7 Pillars of Sustainability

Seven interconnected thematic pillars addressing Borneo’s most critical sustainability challenges and opportunities.

🌿
Pillar 01

Green Economy & Investment

Scaling green finance, sustainable business models, and investment frameworks to unlock Borneo’s green economic potential.

⚡
Pillar 02

Renewable Energy Transition

Accelerating the shift to clean energy — hydropower, solar, and hydrogen — aligned with Sarawak’s SET-P and 2030 targets.

🌿
Pillar 03

Nature & Carbon Solutions

Harnessing Borneo’s forests and biodiversity for nature-based solutions, carbon markets, and conservation finance.

⚖️
Pillar 04

ESG Governance & Policy

Strengthening regulatory frameworks, corporate governance, and public sector policy for sustainable development.

🏪
Pillar 05

SMEs & Community Development

Empowering small businesses and local communities to participate in and benefit from Borneo’s green economy.

💡
Pillar 06

Innovation, Technology & Data

Leveraging cutting-edge technology, ESG data analytics, and innovation ecosystems to drive sustainable outcomes.

🔍
Pillar 07

ESG Accountability & Greenwashing

Ensuring credible, transparent ESG reporting and holding organisations accountable to their sustainability commitments.
