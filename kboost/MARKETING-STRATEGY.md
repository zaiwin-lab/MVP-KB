## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Persona — "The Hustling Owner"
- **Who:** SME owner, 35–50 years old, running a 3–15 person business
- **Revenue:** RM200k–RM1.5M/year
- **Pain:** Works 12-hr days, no sales system, revenue inconsistent
- **Behavior:** Heavy WhatsApp user, mobile-first, trusts word of mouth
- **Language:** BM-dominant or bilingual
- **Location:** Kuching, Miri, Sibu, Bintulu
- **Industries:** Catering, Events, Contractors, Property Agents, Trainers, Consultants

### Secondary Persona — "The Growing Manager"
- **Who:** Sales/BD manager at a 20–50 person SME
- **Pain:** Team hitting limits, no CRM, proposals take too long
- **Goal:** Systematise sales without hiring expensive tools/staff
- **Decision:** Can influence purchase (Growth/Elite plan)

### Excluded Segments (Not yet)
- Retail businesses (product-based, not service-based)
- Large enterprises (>100 staff)
- Kuala Lumpur / West Malaysia (Phase 2)

## 3. Marketing Channels

### Tier 1 — Primary (Launch Focus)

#### A. WhatsApp Marketing (Highest ROI for Sarawak)
- **Tactic:** WhatsApp broadcast list to existing business networks
- **Message:** Short video (30s) showing Dashboard + one result
- **CTA:** "Try 14 days free. No card needed. WhatsApp us."
- **Cadence:** 1 broadcast/week, max
- **Target:** Personal network → business associations → pilot users' networks

#### B. Facebook & Instagram (Local Targeting)
- **Budget:** RM500–1,500/month (launch)
- **Targeting:** 
  - Location: Kuching, Miri, Sibu, Bintulu
  - Age: 28–55
  - Interest: Entrepreneurship, Business, SME Malaysia
  - Behaviour: Small business owners, Business Page admins
- **Ad formats:**
  - Video ad (30-60s): "See KBOOST in 60 seconds" — dashboard walkthrough
  - Carousel: Problem → Solution → Result (before/after)
  - Lead form ads: "Get free guide: How Sarawak SMEs close 2× more deals"
- **Monthly target:** 50–100 demo requests at RM10–30 cost per lead

#### C. LinkedIn (for B2B/Corporate)
- **Tactic:** KOBIS Berhad company page + founder personal posts
- **Content:** AI in Malaysian SME business, Sarawak entrepreneur stories
- **Target:** Company directors, managers, cooperative CEOs

### Tier 2 — Growth (Month 2+)

#### D. Industry Associations & Chambers
- **Targets:**
  - SCCI (Sarawak Chinese Chamber of Commerce)
  - SME Corp Sarawak
  - MBKS / DBKU Business Units
  - SEDA, SEDC-linked programs
  - Bumiputera business associations (PERNAS, etc.)
- **Tactic:** Offer free workshop: "AI Tools for Sarawak SME Sales Growth"
- **Outcome:** Credibility + bulk sign-ups + media coverage

#### E. Content Marketing (SEO + Authority)
- **Blog topics:**
  - "How Sarawak SMEs can generate 500 leads per month (free guide)"
  - "WhatsApp vs AI: Which grows your business faster in 2026?"
  - "Top 6 industries in Sarawak that need a sales system"
- **Video content:** TikTok + YouTube Shorts (BM)
  - "Cara dapat 100 prospek dalam 1 hari — tanpa call"
  - "KBOOST demo — 60 seconds setup"

#### F. Referral Program
- **Mechanic:** Existing KBOOST user refers a new SME → both get 1 month free
- **Target:** Every pilot user becomes a referral source
- **Activation:** WhatsApp referral message template provided

### Tier 3 — Phase 2 (Month 4+)

#### G. Marketplace Network Effect
- Once 50+ SMEs are on KBOOST, activate the Marketplace
- Each SME can list their services → find clients inside the network
- This creates a viral loop: "Join KBOOST to get more clients" becomes even more powerful
- **Mechanic:** "KBOOST Marketplace Listing" as a Growth/Elite benefit

#### H. Partner Channel
- **Target partners:** Accountants, Business Coaches, Digital Agencies, HR Consultants
- **Offer:** 20% recurring commission for every client they refer
- **Tool:** White-label demo + co-branded proposal template

## 6. Pricing & Conversion Strategy

### Current Pricing Analysis
| Plan | Price | Monthly Revenue @ 50 clients | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | RM299 | RM14,950 | Risk: users can't feel full value with 3 proposals/mo |
| Growth | RM799 | RM39,950 | Ideal conversion target |
| Elite | RM1,999 | RM99,950 | Aspirational; requires trust |

### Recommended Pricing Tweaks
1. **Annual plan:** Offer 10 months for 12 months (16% off) — increases LTV and reduces churn
2. **Starter upgrade:** Remove 3-proposal limit (make it 10/month) to improve trial → paid conversion
3. **Launch pricing urgency:** "First 50 clients only at this price" — creates FOMO without being dishonest
4. **Enterprise/Gov tier:** "Custom plan for cooperatives and government agencies" — add CTA for GLCs

### Trial-to-Paid Conversion Tactics
- Day 1: Onboarding message (BM + EN) — help them find first 100 leads
- Day 3: Share first result — "You now have X leads. Here's how to outreach."
- Day 7: Check-in — show them the Proposal Engine
- Day 10: Urgency message — "Your trial ends in 4 days. Here's what you'll lose access to..."
- Day 14: Upgrade offer — "Special rate if you upgrade today"
